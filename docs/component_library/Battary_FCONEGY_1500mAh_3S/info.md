# Battery: FCONEGY 1500mAh 3S 80C

## Overview
Lithium polymer battery used as the main power source for Shadow.

## Specifications
- Type: LiPo
- Capacity: 1500mAh
- Cells: 3S
- Nominal Voltage: 11.1V
- Full Voltage: 12.6V
- Discharge Rate: 80C

## Physical
- Mass: ~136g
- Dimensions: ~70 x 35 x 25 mm

## Connectors
- Main: XT60
- Balance: JST-XH

## System Role
- Supplies power to ESCs and motors

## Integration
- Mounted on bottom of frame with strap belt
- Major contributor to center of mass
